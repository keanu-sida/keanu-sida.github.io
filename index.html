About Me

I am a Charleston, SC-based full stack engineer and data scientist. I graduated from the University of South Carolina with a Bachelor of Science in Biochemistry and Molecular Biology. Upon graduation, I worked at the Medical University of South Carolina in the Mulholland Lab, where I was a Research Scientist. In this position, I developed an affinity for the use of software in solving complex problems in the realm of neuroscience, including in analyzing tissue (through the OpenBrain library within R) and in utilizing image recognition to automate otherwise time-consuming classification tasks in the study of mouse behavior. I have completed a few other projects in bioinformatics and am using this site to showcase some of those.
